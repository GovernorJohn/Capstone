About Us

Flexi Groceries was born out of the idea of necessity to have a modern-day market for our desired international groceries. As an immigirant in St. Louis, John loves his home food and as such always want to get it to cook, but there's a scaricty of his local food items and commodities in the USA, due to lack of proper chanel between the sellers abroad, and buyers in the US.

His website is all here to solve this long existed problem, creating a market for foreign groceries in the US, and helping to supply the needs of the foreigners to their doorsteps.
